ABOUT

"Praphul Marg Darshan" offers classes from grades 1 to 10, providing a well-rounded education that emphasizes academic excellence and holistic development. With dedicated faculty and interactive teaching methods, the school aims to nurture students' growth from foundational skills to preparation for higher education, ensuring they are equipped for success in their future endeavors.

CCTV CAMERA

At Praphul Marg Darshan Kendra, CCTV cameras serve as silent protectors, ensuring safety and preserving the sanctity of this spiritual school. They watch over every corner, offering assurance to visitors and recording timeless moments of devotion and reflection. In this haven of peace, CCTV cameras stand as integral components, embodying the Kendra's commitment to security and spiritual journey.

TOILETS

"Praphul Marg Darshan" prioritizes the safety and comfort of its students by providing separate toilets for girls and boys. This initiative ensures privacy, hygiene, and promotes a conducive learning environment for all students. By maintaining separate facilities, the school aims to uphold the dignity and well-being of each student while fostering a sense of respect and equality. This practice reflects the school's commitment to creating a supportive and inclusive space where every student feels valued and secure.

LOW FEE

"Praphul Marg Darshan" is a school known for its commitment to providing quality education at a low fee. Founded on the principle of making education accessible to all, it ensures that financial constraints do not hinder a child's learning journey. By keeping fees affordable, the school aims to cater to families from diverse socioeconomic backgrounds, fostering inclusivity and equal opportunities for all students. This approach not only promotes education but also empowers communities by breaking barriers to learning.
